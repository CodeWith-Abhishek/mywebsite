My Story

### Abhishek Kumar Mishra: A Journey Fueled by Curiosity and Innovation Hailing from the vibrant city of Nepalgunj in the Banke district of Nepal, Abhishek Kumar Mishra's journey into the world of technology began at a young age. Growing up, he was always fascinated by the gadgets and computers that surrounded him. This early interest in technology laid the foundation for his academic and professional pursuits. Abhishek completed his early education at Golden ABC Academy Banke (High School) in Banke, where he developed a strong academic base. Eager to dive deeper into the sciences, he moved to the capital city, Kathmandu, to pursue his +2 in Science at Trinity SS/College. It was during these two years in Kathmandu that Abhishek's passion for technology grew even stronger, and he decided to pursue a career in this ever-evolving field. His quest for knowledge and excellence led him to KIIT University in Bhubaneswar, India, where he is currently in his third year of a B.Tech degree in Computer Science and Engineering. Here, Abhishek is not only honing his technical skills but also exploring his love for coding and software development. He is currently focused on web development, continuously acquiring new skills and staying updated with the latest technological advancements. Abhishek's journey is not just about academics and technology. He is an avid traveler who loves exploring new places, especially natural landscapes like those in his home country, Nepal. His passion for travel complements his drive for learning, as he believes that new experiences and environments fuel creativity and innovation. Looking ahead, Abhishek is excited to delve into the fields of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, areas that have always intrigued him. With a strong foundation in web development and a keen interest in AI/ML, he is poised to make significant contributions to the tech industry. Abhishek is a young technologist with a bright future, driven by curiosity, a love for exploration, and a deep-seated passion for technology.
